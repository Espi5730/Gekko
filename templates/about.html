About Us

Geeko is an innovative tool created to make the stock market more accessible and less intimidating for college students, particularly those from underrepresented backgrounds. This application offers real-time data, aiding users in better understanding the market. Developed as part of the 2024 SEO Tech Developer program, Geeko seeks to support a diverse group of students in taking their initial steps into investment.
